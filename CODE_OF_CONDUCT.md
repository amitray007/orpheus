# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, everyone who
participates in Orpheus — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other
channel — agrees to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Accept feedback gracefully; give feedback in good faith.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Show empathy toward other participants.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind.
- Sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Personal or political attacks, trolling, or insulting comments.
- Publishing others' private information (doxxing) without explicit permission.
- Any other conduct that would be reasonably considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any other official communication channels associated with Orpheus.
It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
to the project owner at [hey@amitray.dev](mailto:hey@amitray.dev). All reports will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially. The project owner reserves
the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other
contributions that violate this code of conduct, or to ban any contributor whose
behavior is deemed harmful.
